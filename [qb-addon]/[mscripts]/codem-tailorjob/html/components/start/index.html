TAILORJOB TUTORIAL

STEP 1

After starting the business from the lady go to inside of your office.

Open the laptop and give your shop a name.

In the laptop you can get orders from the orders section.

After you get the orders its time to produce clothes.

STEP 2

To produce clothes you’ll need materials.

Go to material shop section on the laptop.

Order the materials you need it for your order.

After you ordered the materials they will go to shop’s storage.

STEP 3

Get the materials from the storage place in your shop

Than go to any sewing machine and produce the clothes you want.

After the production go to your laptop and open the orders section.

Than click on ship order to deliver orders.

STEP 4

Than go to your storage location and pack the orders.

After packing go outside you’ll see shops VAN.

Go back on the VAN and load the packs in there.

Go to the location that set on your map and deliver the package. DONE!
